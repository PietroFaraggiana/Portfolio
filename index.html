Welcome!

This is my personal web page. It's new and still very much work in progress, any critique is welcome! :)

I'm an Italian student with a strong passion for hardware electronics. When I began studying physics, my goal was to become a theoretical physicist. However, my interest shifted toward electronics, which led me to change my academic path.

After completing my Bachelor's in Physics, I initially enrolled in a Master's program in Physics, but I later decided to pursue a master's in Electronics Engineering instead—expected to be completed in 2026.

My main fields of interest are quantum computing and AI accelerators. While my current knowledge in these areas comes from personal research, I plan to study them in-depth during the second year of my master's program.

Beyond academics, my all-time passion is track and field. Throughout my bachelor's and master's studies, I dedicated over 20 hours per week to training, and I achieved fifth place in the 100m event at the 2024 Italian University Championship.

I also enjoy skiing whenever I get the chance, though it's mainly a recreational hobby.
